What is Ticket Buddy?

Ticket Buddy is a website that allows you to find people to go to gigs with, wether none of your friends want to go with you, or maybe you just want to meet new, like minded people.

How does it work?

Use the search bar to enter a venue or artists name. We will then try to match your search results a best we can. All you have to do is select an event and view all the buddies attending. You can view their profiles, send then messages or even send them a buddy request to request to go to a gig with them.

Can I buy tickets?

No. We do not sell tickets on our website, you will either need to have a spare ticket, or find another buddy that has a spare ticket. Alternatavely, you can find a buddy on here and then arrange to buy tickets together from another website.
